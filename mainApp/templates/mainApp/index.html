About us

We believe that the best defense is a strong password. The problem is: the strongest passwords consist of large combinations of numbers, uppercase letters, lowercase letters, and if possible, special characters (e.g., %, #, ^, &, @). All these shared factors eventually give way a more random string that is nearly impossible to memorize and effectively use. The Sketchpass sidesteps all of these problems by allowing you to generate complex passwords that are only accessible through your sketch-based password; A uniquely strong password that can be drawn through various colors and patterns only easily remembered by you.

Have you been compromised?

Benjamin Franklin, one of America’s founding fathers once stated: “By failing to prepare, you are preparing to fail”. The idea being that, preparation is a crucial element for any scenario is pertinent when applying a safeguarded password to avoid a costly mistake. Compromised user credentials can be expensive and time consuming to recover from. It is best to prepare for the worst and to be better safe than sorry. More often than not cyber criminals have a malicious intent when attempting a breach into your personal data. Password compromises can have several sever repercussions that include, but are not limited to:

  • Identity Theft
  • Credit Fraud
  • Personal Information Revealed
  • Tax Fraud
