About us

The Sketchpass is a password manager that allows you to store, organize, and generate complex passwords for any of your favorite websites. The pain of misplacing or forgetting passwords is left behind when you can securely access them through your own unique sketch-based password that is drawn on a grid with a variety of color options for added security. A personalized image drawn by you is much easier to remember and harder to crack than any combination of characters. Give us a try and register today!

Have you been compromised?

Benjamin Franklin, one of America’s founding fathers once stated: “By failing to prepare, you are preparing to fail”. The idea being that, preparation is a crucial element for any scenario is important when applying a safeguarded password to avoid a costly mistake. Compromised user credentials can be expensive and time consuming to recover from. It is best to prepare for the worst and to be better safe than sorry. More often than not cyber criminals have a malicious intent when attempting a breach into your personal data. Password compromises can have unfortunate outcomes that include, but are not limited to:

  • Identity Theft
  • Credit Fraud
  • Personal Information Revealed
  • Tax Fraud
